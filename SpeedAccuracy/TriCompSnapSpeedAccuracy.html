Triangle completion study

Please read the following information carefully before proceeding.

Why we are doing this research: In this project, we aim to study how people infer the position of a triangle's top corner location and angle size.
With this data we hope to learn more about the innate geometry intuitions we have and the mechanisms we use to employ them

What will you have to do: You'll be shown the base of a triangle with its two bottom corner angles. We ask you to position the red dot at the position of the top corner within a time limit.

Benefits and compensations: We do not expect any direct benefits to you from your taking part in this research.
You will be compensated as stated on the Amazon mTurk HIT page or the SONA study pool website for your participation in this study.

Potential risks: You may experience a little discomfort associated with trying to find the location of the top corner or setting the angle.
You are free to leave at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.

Privacy: Your responses will be kept anonymous and stored indefinitely on our password-protected server.
The study does not collect any direct or indirect individual identifiers throughout, with the exception of the SONA systems account ID for purposes of payment recording.
Your SONA systems account ID is kept on a different file from the data-set and will be deleted upon payment and reconciliation with the study coordinator.

Duration: Approximately 30 minutes. Each trial has a time limit of 10 seconds.
